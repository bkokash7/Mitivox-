Frequently Asked Questions

Is Mitivox a subscription?
No. Mitivox is a one-time purchase — pay once and it's yours. No recurring charges, no subscription tiers, no surprise billing.
Can I use it on multiple devices?
Yes. Sign in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase and re-download the app at no extra cost on any of your personal Apple devices.
How do I restore my purchase?
Open the App Store, find Mitivox, and tap the download icon — it'll appear free if your Apple ID already has the purchase. You can also use the "Restore Purchases" option in the app's settings if available.
Something isn't working — what do I do?
Try force-quitting and relaunching the app, then restarting your device. Make sure you're on the latest iOS and the latest version of Mitivox. Still broken? Email us at info@mitivox.com with your device model and iOS version and we'll sort it out.
How do I request a refund?
Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in with your Apple ID to request one. We can't process refunds directly, but email us if you need help navigating the process.
Does Mitivox work with Apple Watch?
Yes. Mitivox includes full Apple Watch support so you can control playback from your wrist without touching your phone.
Who makes Mitivox?
Mitivox is built and published by Seven Kilo Enterprises LLC — an independent software company incorporated in Florida, USA. Seven Kilo Enterprises LLC is the parent holding company (not a DBA) and develops a portfolio of iOS applications directly under that legal entity.
Will the app always be available?
We intend to keep Mitivox available, but can't guarantee it forever. As noted in our Terms of Use, the app may be removed or discontinued at any time. If you've purchased it, keep it installed on your device. See the Terms of Use for the full availability disclaimer.
