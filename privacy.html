Privacy Policy

Effective: May 1, 2026  ·  Last Updated: May 2026
App: Mitivox for iOS  ·  Developer: Seven Kilo Enterprises LLC (Florida, USA)

Seven Kilo Enterprises LLC ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what Mitivox collects, how it's used, and your rights.

Short version: Mitivox doesn't require an account. We don't sell your data. Apple handles all payment processing. We collect minimal information and only to keep the app working well.

1. Who We Are

Mitivox is a product of Seven Kilo Enterprises LLC — a limited liability company incorporated in the State of Florida, USA. Seven Kilo Enterprises LLC is the legal entity (not a trade name or DBA) that develops and publishes this app and others in its portfolio.

Privacy questions: info@mitivox.com

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

Mitivox requires no account and asks for no personal information to use the app. If you email us at info@mitivox.com, we receive whatever you include in that message — solely to respond to you.

Automatically Collected Data

Certain technical data may be collected automatically, including:

  • Device type, OS version, and app version
  • Crash reports and diagnostic logs (to identify and fix bugs)
  • Aggregate usage patterns — not linked to your identity

This flows through Apple's built-in frameworks (crash reporting, app analytics) if you've opted in to share analytics with developers in your iOS Settings. We don't install third-party tracking SDKs.

Payment Data

Mitivox is a one-time App Store purchase. We never see, process, or store your payment details. All transactions are handled entirely by Apple.

3. How We Use It

  • Operate and improve the app
  • Diagnose crashes and bugs
  • Respond to your support emails
  • Meet legal obligations

We do not use your data for advertising, behavioral profiling, or data brokering.

4. Sharing

We don't sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Limited sharing only occurs in these cases:

  • Apple Inc. — as the distribution platform, Apple collects its own data under its own policies.
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, court order, or government authority.
  • Business transfer — in a merger or acquisition, data may transfer under the same protections described here.

5. Data Retention

Email correspondence is kept only long enough to resolve your inquiry. Crash and diagnostic data is retained briefly to address technical issues. We don't maintain long-term profiles on individual users.

6. Children's Privacy

Mitivox is not directed at children under 13. We don't knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Contact us at info@mitivox.com if you believe this has occurred and we'll delete that information promptly.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information we hold. Email info@mitivox.com and we'll respond promptly. Florida residents and residents in states with applicable privacy laws have additional rights which we honor.

8. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect any data we hold. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we can't guarantee absolute security.

9. Third-Party Links

The app or this site may link to third-party services like the App Store. We're not responsible for their privacy practices — review their policies before submitting personal information.

10. Changes

We may update this policy at any time. The "Last Updated" date at the top reflects the most recent change. Material changes will be communicated via in-app notice or App Store update notes. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
